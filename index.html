Development Work Plan & Methodology

Authority: DoED

1. Survey Licensing

Apply for a survey license via the Online Application System. Methodology involves identifying project sites via the GON Project Bank [1, 2].

Authority: MoFE

2. Environmental Clearance

Conduct EIA/IEE studies. Navigate National Forest Land Acquisition if using forest territory [3, 4].

Authority: ERC / NEA

3. Financial PPA

Sign a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA). ERC regulates winter/summer tariffs and IPO/Right Share issues [5, 6].

Authority: DoED

4. Generation License

Apply for a Generation License after technical clearance. Begin site preparation and mobilization [1].
